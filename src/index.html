Hi! My name is Florence Lo and I am a multidisciplinary designer based in Wellington, New Zealand

I originally solely practiced graphic design but due to my increasing curiosty with the digital creative industry I had to explore and find out more! From my studies and work experience I have had the opportunity to explore and develop skills in multiple digital practices, which allowed me to approach client solutions multiple ways.

The style of my work is predominantly vector based, therefore I am able to produce work which is clean, simple and comtemporary allowing for beautiful and effective designs.

As a multidisciplinary designer, I can provide creative solutions using various digital practices to fit your needs.
